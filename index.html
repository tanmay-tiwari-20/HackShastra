HackShastra Pre-Meetup 2025

On 27th July 2025, HackShastra hosted its highly anticipated Pre-Meetup at IIIT-Delhi, an exclusive, high-energy build-up to the HackShastra Hackathon 2025 scheduled for 20th September. What was meant to be a warm-up quickly transformed into a roaring success, reflecting the pulse of our community, the scale of HackShastra, and the impact we’re building together.

Within just 7 days of launch, the Pre-Meetup crossed 1,400+ registrations on Luma, with only 350 carefully selected attendees making it to the final list. And the best part? Over 295+ people showed up physically, filling the venue to capacity. From school students to seasoned professionals, from Kashmir to Kolkata, this wasn’t just another tech event, it was a movement in motion.
